For generating LIME explanations of ML models, you can use text dataset, provided by the organizers of hackaton here: https://zp.edu.ua/vkiit/
For graphical data for MobileNetV2 models you should download one(or all) of these:
- full dataset: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1SHVYfXI35Syc5p0m_H3Zkvt5Tkl0UN5j/view?usp=sharing
- intersection reduced dataset: https://drive.google.com/file/d/16PLqL6hCihw3SxkOfKIFFhVSlmMwCj0m/view?usp=sharing
- union reduced dataset: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1mRrJfkBpKCEGvanvX9oN6tdwP1XuLh0Q/view?usp=sharing
